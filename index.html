Empowering Kisumu’s Children Through Books & Technology

Support Our Mission

Bridging the Digital Divide & Promoting Literacy

The Sulwe Mobile Library is an initiative dedicated to providing books, tablets, and tech literacy tools to children in urban and semi-urban areas of Kisumu. We believe every child deserves the chance to read, learn, and explore technology—no matter their background.

  • ✔ Provide access to storybooks and educational materials
  • ✔ Offer basic digital literacy training using tablets and laptops
  • ✔ Promote inclusive learning for all children, including those with disabilities
  • ✔ Foster a culture of storytelling and creativity

Our Programs

📚 Mobile Library

A tuk-tuk stocked with books & digital devices travels through Kisumu, bringing education to children.

💻 Digital Literacy & Tech Training

Providing interactive tablets with Khan Academy Lite, Scratch coding, and Wikipedia Offline for early learners.

🎭 Storytelling & Creativity Workshops

Featuring local mentors and interactive storytelling sessions for children like Zawadi, Achieng, Otieno, and Musa.

Impact Stories

🌟 Achieng – The Aspiring Storyteller

Achieng, 11, loves drawing and storytelling but lacks books and electricity at home. The mobile library gives her access to storybooks, creative writing tablets, and solar-powered reading lamps.

🔧 Otieno – The Future Engineer

Otieno, 13, is curious about electronics but lacks tools. With digital workshops and donated tech kits, he’s learning coding and hardware repair!

🎨 Zawadi – The Young Artist

Zawadi, 9, expresses herself through art and storytelling. She now uses tablets and drawing apps, creating stunning illustrations that bring her ideas to life!

♿ Musa – The STEM Explorer

Musa, 12, loves puzzles and science. The mobile library provides accessible learning tools like text-to-speech software and interactive STEM games!
